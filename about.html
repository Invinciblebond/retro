Our story

Utopoly started with a simple idea: the golden era of browser worlds — blocky avatars, player-run economies, forums full of trade rumors — deserved a modern home. We're an independent project rebuilding that feeling from scratch, retro style, with none of the bloat: open the site, make an avatar, and you're in.

Everything on the platform is powered by our two currencies, Retrobux and Rix. Create your avatar, collect and equip items from the catalog, hunt Limiteds and ULimiteds on the resale market, join groups, climb the forum leaderboard, and build your own experiences with the creator tools — then hang out with the friends you make along the way.

We're in early access, which means the platform grows in the open. New features ship straight to everyone, the community shapes what comes next, and the wiki documents exactly how it all works — no secrets, no dark patterns.
